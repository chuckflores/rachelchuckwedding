Our Story

September 17th, 2011. We met under the lasers of a Ghostland Observatory show in NYC. After the concert, we split a cab with our friends to a Brooklyn dive bar, The Levee.

In true romantic fashion, we ate cheeseballs, played Connect Four, and Rachel showed off her competitive spirit.

We dated, fell in love, and adopted a (sometimes) sweet cat, Gilda.

Fast Forward to December 31st 2016: Chuck proposed at a captivating frozen waterfall in Sweden.

On June 17th 2017 we married at an intimate ceremony with our family in North Carolina.

On our one year anniversary, we are thrilled to celebrate with our extended family and friends. Thank you for your support and love.
